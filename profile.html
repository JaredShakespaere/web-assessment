My Career

In my years of being a personal trainer, I have found that my niche, or area of specialty, is training athletes. I used to train athletes in person, but I am switching my focus to the online sector for a few reasons.

  1. I can reach more clientele

    2. This makes it easier for me to scale my business.

  2. It helps my athletes be more self-sufficient

    3. When my athletes have to workout alone and they have great remote coaching, they are able to pick up cues a lot quicker than when I hold their hand through the workout.

  3. Better work-life balance

    4. Normal hours for a trainer are crazy. Think of the time you normally go to the gym. It's usually in the morning or at night right? Well as a trainer, I need to be in the gym at the same time as my clientele. So, that usually means I have a split schedule of 4 hours of work in the morning and 4 in the evening. It gets draining after sometime.
